About the Project

ChessOCR is an advanced chess digitization tool that seamlessly converts physical chess data into digital formats. It leverages Machine Learning (ML) and AI to enhance recognition accuracy and improve scanned results over time.

  1. Position Recognition – Converts chessboard images into standard FEN notation.
  2. Handwritten Notation OCR – Scans handwritten chess moves from scoresheets and converts them into PGN format.
  3. AI-Powered Accuracy Check – Uses ML models to validate scanned results and refine accuracy for better digital conversion./li>

By integrating AI-driven enhancements, ChessOCR ensures precise game digitization, making it easier for players to analyze, study, and improve their chess performance.

Project Goals

  • Scan chess board images and convert positions to standard notation
  • Recognize handwritten moves from player scoresheets
  • Convert both board positions and move sequences to PGN format
  • Enable players to easily digitize their physical games for analysis
  • Support various image sources (photos, scans, screenshots)
  • Maintain high accuracy across different handwriting styles
  • Provide a seamless workflow from image scanning to analysis-ready PGN
