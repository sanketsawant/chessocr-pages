About the Project

ChessOCR is a comprehensive chess digitization tool with dual functionality:

  1. Recognizes chess positions from images and converts them to standard notation
  2. Scans handwritten chess moves from scoresheets and converts them to PGN format

This tool aims to bridge the gap between physical chess (boards and scoresheets) and digital analysis, allowing players to easily digitize their games for further study and improvement.

Project Goals

  • Scan chess board images and convert positions to standard notation
  • Recognize handwritten moves from player scoresheets
  • Convert both board positions and move sequences to PGN format
  • Enable players to easily digitize their physical games for analysis
  • Support various image sources (photos, scans, screenshots)
  • Maintain high accuracy across different handwriting styles
  • Provide a seamless workflow from image scanning to analysis-ready PGN

Features

Board Position Recognition

Automatically detects and identifies chess positions from board images

Handwritten Move Recognition

Scans and interprets chess notation written by players on scoresheets

PGN Conversion

Generates standard PGN files compatible with all chess analysis software

Error Detection

Identifies potential errors in move sequences and position legality

Book Readers
